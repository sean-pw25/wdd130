Oregon

Oregon sits on the western side of the United States sandwiched between Washington State on the North, and California on the South. In stark contrast to the fast-paced urban environment of the wet, lush coast on the West, Eastern oregon contains many small and tight-knit communities that exist in a high desert. Sage brush and cattle often populate the hills and valleys here and make for a lovely rural scenery. Small towns on the eastern side of Oregon include Baker City, La Grande, and John Day. Whether you are vacationing or just traveling on Interstate 84, you are likely to see many beautiful sights on your travels through this calm, mountainous region.
