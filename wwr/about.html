Rapids White Water Rafting

Here at Rapids White Water Rafting, we are dedicated to providing safe, fun experiences for all who would like to participate in this adventurous sport.

History

Rapids white water rafting was founded by Jaron Watson in 2009. His love for the rapids inspired many to take the plunge and ride the wild rivers of the Pacific Northwest. Since we began, safety has been our number one priority. Throughout the years, we've teamed up with various top of the line manufacturers and experienced rafting professionals to create the best safety equipment to ensure that all who enjoy this sport, whether experienced or brand new, can do so in safety. We have always taken pride in our instructor's abilities to train and provide memorable experiences for everyone regardless of their experience level.
