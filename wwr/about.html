Rapids White Water Rafting

Here at Rapids White Water Rafting, we are dedicated to providing safe, fun experiences for all who would like to participate in this adventurous sport.

History

Rapids white water rafting was founded by Jaron Watson in 2009. His love for the rapids inspired many to take the plunge and ride the wild rivers of the Pacific Northwest.

We have always taken pride in our instructor's abilities to train and provide memorable experiences for everyone regardless of their experience level.
